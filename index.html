Our mission

Raise awareness about the issues you care about and take action now by making your style about something that matters.

Through their simple design and clear message, our clothes offer a new platform to make the people around you aware of the current issues that matter to you and instill the will to help. Beyond that, our clothes are a token of real commitment for change as 20% of the price of each clothing item we sell is donated to one of the NGOs we carefully selected for their expertise and certified positive impact on our world.

In addition to its social and environmental goals, matters. has an artistic mission consisting in supporting talented young artists and helping them get the attention they deserve. We partner with these young artists to create embroidered collections representative of their body of work as well as one-of-a-kind original items, which truly are wearable pieces of art. Reinventing simple clothes to encourage self-expression is at the core of our mission.

It is a great concern of ours to reduce our environmental impact to a minimum. Our suppliers use high quality organic fabrics, we embroider every piece of clothing on demand in our atelier in Paris and ship our products in compostable mailers.

The purchase of every single matters. product has an immediate and direct positive impact.
